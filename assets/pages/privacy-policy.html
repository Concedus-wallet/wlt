    • Privacy Policy

    At Concedus, available at https://conceds.com/, safeguarding the privacy of our visitors is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how it is used, and how we handle the data at Concedus.

    If you have any questions or require further details about our Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us.

    This Privacy Policy applies strictly to our online activities and is relevant for visitors of our website regarding the information they share or collect at Concedus. It does not apply to data collected offline or through channels other than this website.

    Consent

    By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

    Information We Collect

    When we ask for personal information, we will explain why we are asking for it and how it will be used at that time.

    If you contact us directly, we may receive additional details such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of your message, and any attachments you provide.

    When registering for an Account, we may ask for contact details like your name, company name, address, email address, and phone number.

    How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

    • Operate and maintain our website
    • Improve, personalize, and expand our website
    • Understand and analyze how you use our website
    • Develop new products, services, and features
    • Communicate with you for customer service, updates, and promotional purposes
    • Send you emails
    • Detect and prevent fraud

    Log Files

    Concedus follows standard log file procedures, which track visitors to websites. Information collected may include IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages, and click numbers. This data is not personally identifiable and is used for trend analysis, site administration, and user tracking.

    Cookies and Web Beacons

    Concedus, like many websites, uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and accessed pages to enhance the user experience by optimizing content based on browser type and other factors.

    Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

    Google, as a third-party vendor, uses DART cookies to serve ads to visitors based on their activity on this and other websites. Visitors can opt-out of DART cookie usage by visiting Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

    Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

    You can refer to the Privacy Policy for each of our advertising partners to learn more about their data handling practices.

    Third-party ad servers use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons to deliver personalized advertisements. They automatically collect your IP address when ads are served. Concedus does not have access to or control over these technologies.

    Third-Party Privacy Policies

    Concedus's Privacy Policy does not cover third-party advertisers or websites. We recommend reviewing their respective Privacy Policies for more details on their data practices and how to opt-out of certain actions.

    You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Visit your browser’s website for more information on cookie management.

    CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

    Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California consumers have the right to:

    • Request that a business disclose the categories and specific personal data collected
    • Request the deletion of their personal data
    • Request that a business not sell their personal data

    If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within one month.

    GDPR Data Protection Rights

    We ensure that you are aware of your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

    • The right to access copies of your personal data (we may charge a small fee)
    • The right to request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
    • The right to request the erasure of personal data under certain conditions
    • The right to restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions
    • The right to object to our processing of your data under certain conditions
    • The right to data portability

    If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond within one month.

    Children’s Information

    We are committed to protecting children while they use the internet. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor and guide their children's online activity.

    Concedus does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided such information, contact us immediately, and we will make every effort to remove it from our records.
