    Import Information

    Most of our orders are shipped with 'Duties & Taxes Paid (DDP)', meaning we cover your country's import taxes & duties for you.

    If DDP is not available for your destination, the order will be shipped DDU, which means you will be responsible for paying any applicable import duties & taxes to the shipping carrier or local authorities. In return, all prices in our shop will be automatically reduced by 20%.

    During checkout, on the shipping method selection page, our webshop will indicate if DDP is available for your destination.
